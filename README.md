# ProofPay

**Decentralized escrow for secure freelance payments.**

## Overview
ProofPay is a blockchain‑based escrow platform designed to make freelance payments secure, transparent, and trustless. By leveraging smart contracts, it eliminates reliance on personal trust or intermediaries. Funds are deposited upfront, locked on‑chain, and released only when agreed work is completed—ensuring protection for both clients and freelancers.

## Features
- 🔒 **Trustless Escrow** – Payments are secured by smart contracts, not middlemen.  
- 🌍 **Global Ready** – Seamless cross‑border transactions without delays or currency barriers.  
- ⚡ **Instant Settlement** – Funds are released automatically upon task completion.  
- 🛡️ **Fraud Protection** – Prevents disputes, payment delays, and non‑delivery risks.  
- 📊 **Transparent Ledger** – Every transaction is verifiable on‑chain.  

## Why It Matters
Freelancers often face late payments, disputes, or fraud. ProofPay solves these issues by acting as a reliable payment layer, enabling freelancers and clients worldwide to collaborate with confidence.
